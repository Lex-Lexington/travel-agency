KYRGYZSTAN - KAZAKHSTAN - UZBEKISTAN

Unforgettable memories in three different countries

DAYS WAYPOINT SUBJECTS OF ITINERARY
DAY 1 Tashkent Bishkek Cholpon Ata (280 km) Issyk Kul Arrival in Tashkent.Transfer to the transit hall. Connection Flight to Bishkek.Arrival in Bishkek. Meeting with a guide and driver. Transfer to Cholpon Ata town. Lunch en route. Accommodation in Karven hotel. Overnight and dinner in Karven hotel on the north shore of Issyk Kul Lake. Dinner.
DAY 2 Cholpon Ata Bishkek Breakfast. After breakfast transfer to Bishkek city. En route visit Burana Tower historical complex. Upon arrival half day city tour. Lunch. Visiting State History Museum, Ala Too Square, Kurmanzhan Datka Monument, main governmental buildings, and finish at the Old Square with Parliament House and the only survived in Central Asia Lenin’s Monument. Dinner in a local restaurant. Overnight in a hotel.
DAY 3 Bishkek Tamgaly Almaty (430 km) Breakfast. Morning transfer to Almaty city. En route visit reserve-museum “Tamgaly”. Excursion. Lunch en route. Continue drive to Almaty. Upon arrival accommodation in a hotel. Dinner in a local restaurant/hotel.
DAY 4 Almaty Charyn Canoyn Almaty 400 km Breakfast. All day travel to Charyn canyon. Visiting Valley of Castles, unusual rock formations. Lunch. Return back. Dinner in Indian restaurant. Overnight in a hotel.
DAY 5 Almaty Tashkent Breakfast. Morning full day city tour. Panfilov’s Park with Zenkov’s Orthodox Cathedral, Great Patriotic War Memorial and National Musical Instruments Museum. Lunch. Continue sightseeing tour at Republican Square and State History Museum. Visiting Medeo world famous skating ring, situated in a mountain gorge near the city. Late evening transfer to airport to depart to Tashkent. Dinner in a local restaurant in Tashkent.
DAY 6 Tashkent Breakfast in the hotel. Sightseeing tour in Tashkent: Khast Imam Complex, including the Mausoleum of Kaffal Ash Shashi, Barak Khan Madrassah, Imam Al Bukhari High Religious School, Tilla Sheikh Mosque, Museum of Islamic Manuscripts. Lunch. Shopping tour: Visiting Chorsu Bazaar (National Uzbek Dresses & dry fruit and nuts), Central Department Store (TSUM). Abdul Kasym Madrassah Handicraft Center. Dinner.
DAY 7 Tashkent Chimgan-Charvak Tashkent Breakfast. Transfer to Chimgan mountains. Chair lift to reach on top of the mountain (30 min). Walk around and enjoy the mountain scenery and also take the breathtaking pictures of the mountains. Drive to Charvak (a unique Artificial water reservoir). Lunch in “Charvak Pyramids (the Hotel Pyramids). After lunch transfer to Tashkent. Free at leisure. Dinner.
DAY 8 Tashkent Breakfast. Transfer to the airport.
