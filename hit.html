HOTEL INTERNATIONAL TASHKENT

Nothing can express the merits of International Hotel better than its five-stars status will. This is the five-star hotel in Uzbekistan and its name has become the symbol of luxury, respectability and prestige. The International Hotel is situated in the central part of the city where the largest business offices are located. There you can find the Uzexpocenter exhibition gallery, the National Bank, the International Business Center and etc. On the entering the hotel you find yourself in the world of delicacy and elegance. Antique styled furniture, marble and gilding, glass and metal go together in the interior of this place. Life is in full swing in the Intercontinental, various important events such as presentations, banquets, celebrations, conferences, briefings, seminars and trainings are being held here. Business centers and conference halls are always glad to meet and accommodate even a huge number of guests. The best restaurants of the city like the Los Amigos, the Allegro, the Brasseries Park as well as the Intermezzo, the Rendezvous and the Atrium Lobby bars are at your service. You will be delighted by the dishes cooked in these temples of taste. The International Hotel totally has 243 rooms 2 of which are executive deluxe suites, 18 are junior suites and 1 is a presidential suite. Each room is the standard of comfort and coziness. Fashionable furniture, air-conditioning system, international communication facilities, mini-bar with all possible drinks, safe, any wish of yours is guessed and realized beforehand here.
