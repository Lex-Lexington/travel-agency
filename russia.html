GET TO KNOW RUSSIA WITH BHT-TOUR

Beautiful places of Russia

DAYS WAYPOINT SUBJECTS OF ITINERARY
DAY 1 Tashkent St. Petersburg Arrival in Tashkent. Transfer to the Transit Hall. Connection Flight to St. Petersburg. Meeting at the a/port. Transfer to the hotel. Accommodation in the hotel. Dinner at the hotel
DAY 2 St. Petersburg Breakfast in the hotel. Sightseeing tour: Peterhoff Palace &Garden with the guide. Lunch. Walking tour along the Nevski Avenue. Transfer to the river station (the bus waiting at the station). River Cruise along Neva river. Meeting at the river station and transfer for dinner. Dinner. Transfer to the hotel.
DAY 3 St. Petersburg Breakfast in the hotel. Transfer to Tsarskoe Selo. Sightseeing tour: Tsarskoe Selo, including Catherine Palace (with the local guide), which is filled with 18thcentury paintings and ornate rooms of which the famous is the Amber room. Lunch. City tour: Vasilievsky Island. Dinner. Transfer to the hotel.
DAY 4 St. Petersburg Moscow Breakfast in the hotel. Sightseeing tour to Hermitage & Winter Palace. Friday prayer in Grand Mosque, which was built by the order of the Emir of Bukhara. Lunch. Free at leisure. Transfer to StP. Railway Station. Moving to Moscow by train. Dinner in lunchboxes. (On the train). Meeting group in Moscow Railway Station. Transfer and Accommodation in hotel.
DAY 5 Moscow Breakfast. Bus tour of the city. Sightseeing tour to the Kremlin, the Armory, Tsar Cannon, Tsar Bell. Lunch. Continuation of the excursion, visiting Izmaylovskogo vernissage. Tour of the Red Square. Dinner. Transfer to the hotel
DAY 6 Moscow Breakfast. Transfer to airport. Arrival in Tashkent. Connection flight to your city.
DAY 7 Moscow Tashkent Breakfast in the hotel. Morning visit to Abul Kassym medrese. Lunch Khasti Imam Complex, including the Mausoleum of Kaffal Ash-Shashi, Barak Khan Madrassah, Imam Bukhari High Religious School, Tilla Sheikh Mosque, Museum of Islamic Manuscripts. Visit to Kukeldash Madrassah. Dinner.
