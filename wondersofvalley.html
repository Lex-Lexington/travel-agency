WONDERS OF VALLEY

Unforgettable memories in incredible places

DAYS WAYPOINT SUBJECTS OF ITINERARY
DAY 1 Tashkent Arrival in Tashkent. Evening flight to Bukhara. Arrival in Bukhara. Transfer to hotel. Dinner. Overnight in Bukhara.
DAY 2 Bukhara Breakfast in the hotel. Sightseeing tour in Bukhara: Sightseeing tour in Bukhara: Poi-Kalyan Ensemble including Minaret (12c) Mosque (15c), functioning Madrassah Miri-Arab (16 c), Emir-Alim-Khan Madrassah (14c), Taqi (trading domes), Kukeldash Madrassah (16c), Nadir Divanbegi (17c), Magok-i-Attari Mosque (16c), Lyab-i-Hauz (17c). Lunch. Visit to Registan, Ark Fortress (16 c) and Bolo-Hauz complex (18 c). Ulug Beg (15c), Abdul Aziz (17c) Madrassahs; Chor-Minor Mausoleums. Ismael Samani mausoleum (9-10c)-vault of Bukhara rulers dynasty, Chashma-e-Ayub Mausoleums (14 c). Dinner.
DAY 3 Bukhara Breakfast in the hotel. Sightseeing tour out of Bukhara: Visiting Naqshbandi complex, Sitorai-Mokhi-Khosa-“ Summer Palace of Bukhara’s last Emir” Lunch Free at leisure Dinner
DAY 4 Bukhara Samarkand Breakfast in the hotel On the visiting Gijduvan Ceramic and Silk producing workshop. Lunch en route. Transfer to Samarkand. On the way stop to see Malik Rabat water well. Visiting Imam Al Bukhari Complex with the Mosque and Mausoleum. Accommodation in the hotel. Free at leisure Rest after long trip Dinner
DAY 5 Samarkand Breakfast in the hotel. Sightseeing tour in Samarkand: Shah-i-Zinda necropolis (14-15c), “Stars above Samarkand”: tour to Ulugbek observatory (15c). Lunch. Sightseeing tour in Samarkand: Gur-Emir Mausoleum (15c), Registan Ensemble (including Ulugbek (15c), ShirDor (17c), Tillya –Kari Madrassahs (17c), BibiKhanum Mosque Dinner.
DAY 6 Samarkand Tashkent Breakfast in the hotel. Morning tour to famous Samarkand paper mill factory. (A small garden where the oldest and famous Samarkand paper is made). Lunch Visiting the “Afrosiab” State Archaeological Museum. Transfer to Samarkand Railway station Transfer to Tashkent by bullet train “Afrosiab” Arrival in Tashkent. Dinner Accommodation in the hotel.
DAY 7 Tashkent Breakfast in the hotel. Morning visit to Abul Kassym medrese. Lunch Khasti Imam Complex, including the Mausoleum of Kaffal Ash-Shashi, Barak Khan Madrassah, Imam Bukhari High Religious School, Tilla Sheikh Mosque, Museum of Islamic Manuscripts. Visit to Kukeldash Madrassah. Dinner.
DAY 8 Tashkent Fergana Breakfast in the hotel. Rest in the hotel. Check-out. (It would be nice if you leave the unnecessary luggage in Tashkent) Flight to Fergana. Arrival. Lunch. Transfer to Margilan – the center of the Silk. Visiting Silk Producing factory “Yodgorlik”. Dinner.
DAY 9 Fergana Breakfast in the hotel. Depart to Tashkent by car. Transfer to Rishtan (the Center of the Ceramic Pottery). Visiting the Ceramic Studio in Rishtan. Lunch. Transfer to Kokand. Sightseeing tour in Kokand: The Palace of Khudoyar Khan (19-20 cc.) Arrival in Tashkent Accommodation in the hotel. Check In Dinner.
DAY 10 Tashkent Breakfast in the hotel. Lunch Shopping tour in Tashkent: Chor-Su – oriental market (handicraft bazaar). Abul Kassym medrese. Farewell dinner Transfer to Tashkent International Airport.
