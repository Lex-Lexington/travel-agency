THE SILK ROAD

Unforgettable memories in historacal places

DAYS WAYPOINT SUBJECTS OF ITINERARY
DAY 1 Tashkent-Khiva Arrival in Tashkent Transfer and accommodation at the hotel Breakfast Excursion through Tashkent: visit to Hasti Imam Square, including the Museum of Islamic calligraphy, madrassah of Barakhan. Then visit to the old bazaar "Chorsu" Lunch After lunch, visit to museums: Museum of History; Museum of Applied Arts (visit lasts until 16.00). Arrival at hotel Transfer to the airport. Flight to Khiva (18.20) Arrival in Khiva (20.00) Dinner Transfer and accommodation at the hotel Rest after the flight
DAY 2 Khiva Breakfast Excursion through Khiva: Ark Fortress Cunha, Madrassah of Mohammed Amin Khan (XIX century); Islam Khodja Minaret; Complex of Palvan Kari; The complex of Abd al Rahim bobo; Madrassah of Muhammad Rahim Khan; Mausoleum of shoi Kalandar Bobo. Lunch Excursion through Khiva: Mazari Sharif Madrassah; Mausoleum Uch Avliyoli ("Three Saints"); Juma mosque; Mausoleum of Pahlawan Mahmud; Tash Hauvli Palace ("Stone house"); Alla Kuli Khan; Madrassah of Mohammed Khan. Dinner
DAY 3 Khiva-Bukhara Breakfast Transfer to Bukhara in the bus through the desert Kyzyl Kum. On the way, stop at the bank of the Amu Darya, to see one of the main rivers in Central Asia. Lunch Arrival in Bukhara. Free time Dinner
DAY 4 Bukhara Breakfast A visit to the city: A visit to the Mausoleum of Ismail Samani (IX-X century) and "Chashma Ayub"; Fortress "Ark" (the winter residence of Bukhara's emirs). Lunch After lunch, tour through the city: Visit to the architectural complex "Poi Kalon" (including the minaret of XIIth century) Madrassah of Mir Arab (XVI century) Madrassah of Emir Alimkhan Dome Shops Kukaldosh Madrassah (XVI century) Nadir Devan Begi (XVII century) Magoki Attari Labi Hovuz (XVII century) Dinner
DAY 5 Navoi-Bukhara-Samarkand Breakfast Tour outside the city: Chor Minor Naqshbandiy (1 hr.) Sitora Mohi Khosa Chor-Bakr Transfer to Navoi through Gijduvan. A visit to the workshop of craftsmen. Stop in Navoi. Excursion to the well and Karvansaray. Lunch Transfer to Samarkand by bus Arrival in Samarkand Dinner Hotel accommodation.
DAY 6 Samarkand-Tashkent Breakfast City tour in Samarkand: Gur Emir mausoleum (XV century); Registan Ensemble (including Ulugbek (XV century), Sher-Dor, Tillya Kari Madrassah (XVII c)); Bibi Khanum; Tour of Samarkand bazaar. Lunch A visit to the silk carpets producing factory. Continuation of sightseeing in Samarkand: Shahi Zinda, "Stars above Samarkand" visit to Observatory of Ulugbek (XV century). Transfer to Tashkent on high-speed train "Afrosiyob" (17:00) Arrival in Tashkent (19:00) Dinner
DAY 7 Tashkent Breakfast Time for shopping and buying souvenirs: Abul Kasim medrese, Alay Bazaar Accompanying to the airport.
