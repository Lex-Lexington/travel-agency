HOTEL WYNDHAM

Welcome to our 4-star Wyndham Tashkent hotel, the newest upscale international hotel in Tashkent, Uzbekistan and one specifically designed for discriminating travelers who seek personalized, first-class service. Located in the heart of the city, our elegant and hospitable Tashkent hotel is perfectly situated for both business and pleasure travel: Alisher Navoi Metro Station is a mere five-minute walk from our front doors, we are the closest hotel to Tashkent International Airport (TAS), and many of Tashkent’s notable and legendary attractions are within easy reach of our front doors, including the Amir Temur Museum and Alay Bazaar, as well as iconic sites like Independence Square (Mustakillik Square), Chorsu Bazaar, Khast Imam Mosque and the History Museum of the People of Uzbekistan. Our five exquisite event facilities have been created to maximize creativity, success and celebration, and with the assistance of our onsite meetings professionals, your event is guaranteed of success, whether you are hosting a corporate event, press conference or celebrating a special occasion. After a successful gathering, or business meeting, our wealth of leisure facilities beckons, including the signature Wyndham Blue Harmony Spa and health club, a vast array of stress-relieving massage therapies, soothing Turkish hammam, calming Finnish sauna, and sparkling indoor and outdoor pools. Feast on traditional Uzbek cuisine and many international delights onsite at our charming Opera Cafe, where you can enjoy a delicious buffet breakfast each morning, a wide range of a la carte options for lunch and dinner, and all the while, relax to the beautiful and calming sounds of a live harp. Retreat to one of our well-appointed guestrooms, outfitted for ultimate comfort, safety and tranquility with features that include air conditioning, mini-bar, flat-screen HDTV with dozens of satellite channels, and in-room safe, or upgrade to our Executive Rooms and Suites which boast ultimate luxury, expanded space, breathtaking views and a host of thoughtful extras, like free WiFi, available throughout our 4-star hotel in Tashkent. Discover why we are a top choice among discerning travelers seeking elegant international Tashkent hotels. We look forward to welcoming you to Uzbekistan’s capital with warmth, grace and hospitality.
